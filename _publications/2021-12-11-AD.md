The digital economy is characterized by the triadic integration of "data, platform and algorithm", which calls for new legal care different from the anti-monopoly logic of the industrial economy. Among them, how to determine the values of the Law Should Be has become the core issue of unity. Reviewing the development of the antitrust value system, the digital economy antitrust needs not only to adhere to the efficiency orientation, but also to consider encouraging innovation, promoting competition, maintaining security, respecting privacy and other diversified value orientations, so as to avoid the limitation of "death by control and chaos by release" and to achieve the purpose of promoting order by good law.
