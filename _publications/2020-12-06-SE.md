This paper extracts, organizes and analyzes the exploitative discussions on Zhihu platform through technical means, and recognizes that the relevant discussions show an increasingly strong trend in the spatio-temporal dimension in China today. On the basis of some consensus, this paper discusses the exploitation phenomenon in China today from two perspectives: residual value theory and historical materialism. Based on the theory of surplus value, this paper argues that the problem of exploitation is closely related to the problem of value, and that Marx's labor theory of value, formed by Marx's critical inheritance of the value theory of classical political economy and the theory of surplus value developed on its basis, is an important basis for understanding the problem of exploitation. The reconceptualization of Marx's labor theory of value clears some misunderstandings about the theory itself and provides a new perspective for explaining the problem of exploitation under socialism with Chinese characteristics. Based on historical materialism, this paper argues that the level of development of productive forces is an important factor determining the emergence, development and extinction of exploitation, and that exploitation also promotes the development of productive forces at a certain stage, and the understanding of the phenomenon of exploitation must be based on the law of productive forces. At the same time, the problem of exploitation is also necessarily affected by the policy of distribution. However, the "certain inevitability" of exploitation must be re-examined under the guidance of the people's historical view in order to make progress in solving the problem.
