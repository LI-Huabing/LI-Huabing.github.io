Since September 2021, I have had the honor of working as an assistant researcher at the Institute for National Development and Strategic Studies of Renmin University of China. My job is to write policy commentary articles in the form of a collection of "Political and Economic Reference" and "State of the Nation Update", which are submitted to local government departments for internal reference every month. Since the content of the articles is confidential, I cannot present the full text here, but only update the work dynamics once a month for those who need it. If you are interested, you can discuss with me.

2. Will the "Belt and Road" Initiative Promote the Construction of Community in the Countries Along the Route, 2022.02
3. The contemporary orientation of antitrust contingent values in the digital economy, 2022.03
4. Class Affiliation, Class Mobility, and Subjective Well-being: Cracking the "Easterlin" Paradox in China, 2022.04
5. Measurement and Evaluation of the Contribution of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises in the Current National Economy, 2022.05
6. Property Rights Protection, Fair Competition and Industrial Economic Development, 2022.07
